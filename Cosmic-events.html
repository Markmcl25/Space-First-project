What are they?

Cosmic events are awe-inspiring occurrences in the vast expanse of the universe that captivate our imagination and deepen our understanding of the cosmos. These extraordinary phenomena encompass a diverse array of occurrences, from the explosive beauty of supernovae to the intricate dance of planets and moons.
